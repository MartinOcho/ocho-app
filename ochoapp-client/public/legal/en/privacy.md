# Privacy Policy of OchoApp

Effective Date: February 18, 2026

Last Updated: February 18, 2026

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to OchoApp! We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal data. This privacy policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and what your rights are regarding data protection. By using OchoApp, you accept the practices described in this policy. If you disagree, please do not use our application.

## 2. Data We Collect

### Account Information

- **Username and Display Name**: Your chosen username and display name are collected when you create an account and are publicly visible.
- **Email Address**: We collect your email address to enable account creation, password recovery, and important notifications.
- **Password Hash**: We store a secure hash of your password, not the password itself.
- **Birthday**: Optional information used for personalization and to ensure age requirements.
- **Profile Picture/Avatar**: We store your profile picture via Cloudinary and display it on your profile.
- **Bio**: Your optional biographical text is publicly visible.

### Authentication Data

- **OAuth Provider IDs**: If you authenticate via Google, Facebook, or GitHub, we store your ID with those providers to link your account.
- **Session Tokens**: We create and store secure session tokens to keep you logged in.
- **Device Information**: We collect and store:
  - Device type (Android, iOS, Web, Desktop, Unknown)
  - Device model
  - IP address
  - Approximate location (based on IP)

### Social Data

- **Following/Followers**: We store which users you follow and who follows you.
- **Verification Status**: We store whether your account is verified (Standard or Golden tier) and expiration dates.
- **User Privacy Settings**: We store your privacy preferences and blocking/filtering lists.

### Content You Create

- **Posts**: All text, images, videos, and attachments in posts are stored with timestamps and metadata.
- **Comments and Replies**: All comments on posts and replies to comments are stored.
- **Likes**: We record which posts and comments you have liked.
- **Bookmarks**: We store posts you have bookmarked for later viewing.
- **Direct Messages**: All messages between users are encrypted during transmission and stored on our servers.
- **Message Attachments**: Images, videos, and files sent in messages are stored via Cloudinary.
- **Message Reactions**: Emoji reactions to messages are recorded.
- **Message Mentions**: When you mention a user (@username) in a message, we record this for notifications.

### Activity Data

- **Search History**: We store your search queries and when you performed them.
- **Read Receipts**: We track which messages you have read and when.
- **Message Delivery Status**: We track receipt and delivery of messages.
- **Login History**: We maintain records of when and from which devices you log in.
- **Last Seen**: We track your last activity timestamp.
- **Online Status**: We track whether you are currently online.
- **Typing Indicators**: We temporarily track when you are typing in conversations.
- **File Upload Status**: We track when you are uploading files.

### Technical Data

- **IP Address**: Collected from all connections to our service.
- **Browser and Device Information**: Type, version, and operating system.
- **Crash Reports**: Error logs to help us debug issues.
- **Analytics**: Usage patterns, features accessed, and performance metrics.

### Derived Data

- **Engagement Scores**: We calculate relevance scores for posts based on likes, comments, and bookmarks.
- **User Recommendations**: We create audience profiles based on your activity to recommend posts and users.
- **Trend Analysis**: We analyze aggregated data to identify trends and popular topics.

## 3. How We Use Your Data

### Core Service Delivery

- **Account Management**: Maintaining your account, authentication, and session management.
- **Messaging**: Delivering messages, tracking read receipts, managing message history, and storing media attachments.
- **Social Features**: Enabling posts, comments, likes, bookmarks, following, and notifications.
- **Device Security**: Tracking devices on your account to detect unauthorized access.

### Service Improvement

- **Feed Personalization**: Using engagement data to recommend relevant posts and accounts.
- **Search Improvement**: Analyzing search queries to improve search relevance.
- **Feature Development**: Understanding how features are used to develop new features.
- **Performance Monitoring**: Tracking errors and performance to ensure reliability.

### Communication

- **Notifications**: Sending notifications for likes, comments, new followers, messages, and mentions.
- **Updates**: Notifying you of important platform changes and security alerts.
- **Support**: Responding to your inquiries and support requests.

### Safety and Compliance

- **Abuse Detection**: Analyzing activity patterns to detect spam, bot accounts, and abuse.
- **Legal Compliance**: Complying with legal requests from authorities.
- **Fraud Prevention**: Detecting and preventing fraudulent activity.
- **Account Recovery**: Helping you recover access to your account when locked out.

## 4. Message Storage and Encryption

- Messages are encrypted in transit (TLS/SSL).
- Messages are stored in our database on secure servers.
- We retain all messages indefinitely unless you request deletion.
- Deleted messages on your interface may still exist in backups.
- We can access message content for legal compliance, abuse investigation, and customer support.
- We do not delete messages at user request once they are sent.

## 5. Data Sharing

### Public Data

Your posts, comments, username, display name, profile picture, followers/following list, and bio are visible to all users by default. Other users can share, quote, or reference your public posts.

### Third-Party Services

We use third-party services for:
- **Cloudinary**: Image and video hosting and optimization
- **Authentication Providers**: Google, Facebook, GitHub (when you use these to log in)
- **Analytics**: Understanding platform usage patterns
- **Hosting**: Database and application hosting providers

These services have access to limited data necessary to function. We have data processing agreements with all partners.

### Legal Requirements

We may disclose your data if required by law, including:
- Court orders or subpoenas
- Government investigations
- Law enforcement requests
- Protecting safety or legal rights

We will not disclose information without proper legal process unless safety is at immediate risk.

### Business Transfer

If OchoApp is acquired, merged, or sold, your data may be transferred to the new owner. We will notify you of any such change.

### Consent-Based Sharing

We will not share your personal data with third parties beyond those listed above without your explicit consent, except as required by law.

## 6. Data Security

We implement technical and organizational security measures:

- **Encryption in Transit**: All communications use TLS/SSL encryption.
- **Encryption at Rest**: Sensitive data is encrypted in storage.
- **Access Controls**: Only necessary employees can access personal data.
- **Authentication**: We use secure authentication methods.
- **Monitoring**: We monitor systems for suspicious activity and vulnerabilities.
- **Backups**: We maintain secure backups for disaster recovery.
- **Password Hashing**: Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms.

No security system is completely secure. If you believe your data has been compromised, contact us immediately.

## 7. Data Retention

- **Account Data**: Retained while your account is active and for 30 days after deletion (unless required by law for longer).
- **Messages**: Retained indefinitely on our servers.
- **Posts and Comments**: Retained indefinitely unless you delete them (deletion removes them from view but may persist in backups).
- **Logs**: Technical logs retained for 90 days.
- **Analytics**: Aggregated analytics retained indefinitely.
- **Search History**: Retained indefinitely; can be cleared in settings.
- **Device Information**: Retained as long as sessions are active or for recovery purposes.

## 8. Your Rights

In accordance with data protection laws (such as GDPR and CCPA), you have:

- **Right to Access**: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.
- **Right to Correction**: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
- **Right to Deletion**: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements).
- **Right to Data Portability**: Request your data in a structured, commonly used format.
- **Right to Object**: Object to processing of your data for certain purposes.
- **Right to Withdraw Consent**: Withdraw consent for data processing based on consent.
- **Right to Restrict Processing**: Request restriction of data processing in certain circumstances.

To exercise these rights, contact us at: ochokom@ochotouchsolution.onmicrosoft.com

We will respond within 30 days or as required by law.

## 9. Children's Privacy

OchoApp is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we discover a user is underage, we will delete the account and associated data. Parents who believe their child has created an account should contact us immediately.

## 10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred, stored, and processed in countries other than your country of residence. By using OchoApp, you consent to such transfers. We ensure appropriate safeguards including standard contractual clauses.

- Block or mute users
