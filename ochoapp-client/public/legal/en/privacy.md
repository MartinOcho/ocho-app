# Privacy Policy of OchoApp

Effective Date: January 5, 2024

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to OchoApp! We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal data. This privacy policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and what your rights are regarding data protection. By using OchoApp, you accept the practices described in this policy. If you disagree, please do not use our application.

## 2. Data We Collect

When you use OchoApp, we may collect the following information:

- **Full Name** : Your full name is collected to create your user profile. This allows us to personalize your experience and identify you on the platform.

- **Email Address** : We collect your email address to enable you to create an account, reset your password, and receive important notifications.

- **Public Data** : Information you choose to make public, such as your posts, is accessible to all OchoApp users. This includes comments, likes, and shares.

- **Public Posts** : Any content you publish on OchoApp, including text, images, and videos, is stored and may be visible to other users. We recommend that you not share sensitive or personal information in your posts.

- **Profile Picture** : Your profile picture is used to personalize your account and is visible to other users. You can change or delete it at any time.

- **Follower Count** : We collect and display the number of users who follow you on OchoApp. This information is public and part of your profile.

- **Technical Data** : We collect technical information such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, and location data to improve application performance and detect technical issues.

## 3. Use of Data

The data we collect is used for the following purposes:

- **Provide, Maintain and Improve Our Services** : We use your data to ensure OchoApp functions properly, resolve technical issues, and develop new features.

- **Personalize Your User Experience** : Your data helps us personalize your feed, recommend relevant content, and adapt the interface to your preferences.

- **Connect You With Other Users** : We use your information to facilitate social interactions on OchoApp, such as messaging, commenting, and notifications.

- **Analyze OchoApp Usage** : We analyze usage data to understand how users interact with the application and identify areas for improvement.

- **Send Communications** : We may use your email address to send you updates, newsletters, and information about new features. You can unsubscribe at any time.

## 4. Data Sharing

We only share your personal data with your consent or in the following cases:

- **With Third-Party Service Providers** : We work with third parties for hosting, analytics, and maintenance of OchoApp. These partners are bound by strict confidentiality obligations.

- **To Comply With Legal Obligations** : We may share your data if required by law, for example in response to a court order or government investigation.

- **In Case of Merger or Acquisition** : If OchoApp is acquired or merged with another company, your data may be transferred to the new entity. We will inform you of any change of ownership.

## 5. Data Security

We implement technical and organizational security measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

- Encryption of data during transmission and storage.
- Strict access controls to limit access to sensitive data.
- Regular monitoring of our systems to detect security vulnerabilities.

Despite our efforts, no transmission or storage method is completely secure. If you have reason to believe your data has been compromised, please contact us immediately.

## 6. Your Rights

In accordance with data protection laws, you have the following rights:

- **Access Your Data** : You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

- **Correct Your Data** : If your information is inaccurate or incomplete, you can request to have it updated.

- **Delete Your Data** : You can request the deletion of your personal data, subject to certain legal exceptions.

- **Object to Processing** : You can object to the use of your data for reasons related to your particular situation.

- **Withdraw Your Consent** : If we process your data based on your consent, you can withdraw it at any time.

To exercise these rights, contact us at: ochokom@ochotouchsolution.onmicrosoft.com. We will respond to your request within the required timeframes.

## 7. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will inform you of any significant changes by publishing the new policy on this page or sending you a notification. We encourage you to review this policy regularly to stay informed.

## 8. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or wish to exercise your rights, you can contact us at: ochokom@ochotouchsolution.onmicrosoft.com. We are committed to responding to your requests as promptly as possible.
